lion

Lions, iconic predators of the African savannas, symbolize strength and majesty with their distinctive manes and powerful roars. Living in social prides, they contribute significantly to ecological balance. These magnificent creatures face threats from habitat loss and human-wildlife conflict, underscoring the importance of conservation efforts to safeguard both lions and their vital roles within their habitats.

Scientific name: Panthera Leo

Class: Mammalia

Habitat: Grasslands, savannas, and open woodlands

Diet: Carnivora

Life Span: 10 to 14 years

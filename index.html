lion

Lions, the epitome of charismatic megafauna, are iconic members of the Felidae family. Recognized for their majestic manes, powerful roars, and regal presence, these carnivores command attention in the African savannas and some parts of Asia. Social beings, they form prides, where cooperative hunting and communal care of cubs thrive. Lions play a vital ecological role as apex predators, contributing to the balance of their ecosystems. Despite their revered status, they face threats such as habitat loss and human-wildlife conflict, prompting conservation efforts to safeguard these magnificent creatures and the biodiversity they represent in the fragile tapestry of nature.

scientific name: Panthera Leo

Class: Mammalia

Habitat: Grasslands, savannas, and open woodlands

Diet: Carnivora
